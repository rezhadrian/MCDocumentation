◆ IndexToEdges

MC_CONSTEXPR MCindex tables::IndexToEdges[256]

Represents the edges, which are cut by an isosurface in the Cube.

For the __mc_cube_point__ ["cube point"] i retrieves the hexal number IndexToEdges[i] that has 1 at the position of those edges, which are cut by the isosurface, and zero otherwise.
